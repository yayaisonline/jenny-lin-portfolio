Design

Graphic design has been part of my life since high school, where I made hundreds of visuals for student associations and school events — peaking during the Covid era of online everything. On the side, I channel pop culture obsessions into fanart, novel covers, and the occasional attempt to turn a song into a visual. In college, I've taken on remote freelance work in editorial design, and found something I love at the intersection of storytelling and data — where a well-designed graphic doesn't just decorate, but argues. Here is a mix of old and recent work — enjoy.
