Hi, my name is
Jenny Lin.

I am a senior journalism student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, with a minor in Gender and Women's Studies. Originally from Fuzhou, China, I grew up in a vibrant cultural environment and set my goal to pursue work in creative fields from childhood.

My work explores social issues through investigative and multimedia storytelling, with a focus on how policy, inequality, and lived experience shape communities. I am passionate about turning different languages — data, visuals, and coding — into storytelling. Along my practice in journalism, I am also archiving my passion to be a curious learner, designer, and researcher — I believe every path of curiosity and study goes to one room.
